Barometz Balut

Description

A dish prepared by Senshi from an unripe Barometz fruit. The Barometz' ribs are sliced into chops then grilled and steamed, with a sauce made from the skinned, boiled rind of the fruit poured on top.

Ingredients

  • Barometz fruit (unripe; 1)
  • Barometz sprouts (500g)
  • Salt and pepper (as needed)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Wine (2 tablespoons)
