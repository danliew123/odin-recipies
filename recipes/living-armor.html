Living Armor Full Course Meal

Description

A set of dishes using one set of Living Armor prepared four different ways by Senshi - stir-fried, steamed, in a soup and grilled. Eating the parts in the helmet is not recommended as they taste musty.

Steamed Living Armor

Ingredients

  • Living Armor (1, whole in shell)
  • Salt, soy sauce, vinegar (to taste)

Living Armor Soup

Ingredients

  • Living Armor (3-4 large pieces
  • Basilisk egg (1)
  • Medicinal herb (1 bunch)
  • Soy sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Special sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Grilled Living Armor

Ingredients

  • Living Armor (1, whole in shell)
  • Salt, soy sauce, vinegar (to taste)
